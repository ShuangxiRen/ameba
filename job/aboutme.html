About me

Dear Sir/Madam:

I just graduated from Pennsylvania State University (PSU), with a master degree in Computer Science and Engineering. I am now living in Bluffton with my wife who is a faculty in University of South Carolina Beaufort. I am looking for a job as a web developer near Bluffton.

I came into contact with web development in my database course and I found I so love it. Then I learned HTML, CSS, PHP and JavaScript. To demonstrate my knowledge of them, I developed this demo shopping website based on LAMP. Then I purchased a domain name and server resource to show it to my potential employers.

Since I want to make this demo ready before I give out my first resume, I choose to use as short time as possible (less than three weeks) to simply realize the core functions, without paying much attention to the layout and beautification. However, I take into account of preventing XSS attack and SQL injection by validating and sanitizing form data, and by using sql prepared statements.

In order to understand these languanges well, I design the whole project by myself and type down all the source code without using any templates or models.

Following are the main functions of this website and each works well under Chrome.

  • create a user and sign in
  • update your personal information
  • upload your items for sale directly or for auction
  • search for items or browse items by categories
  • add items into your wishlist
  • purchase items
  • bid items
  • maintain the delivery process either as a seller or buyer
  • rate and review other users or get rated or reviewed by other users
  • check your order list
  • check your selling list
  • check reviews you get from other users
  • contact other users through messages
  • get system messages during a purchase or bid behavior
